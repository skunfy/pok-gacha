Privacy Policy

Overview

Gachax is a companion web application for trading card collection and browsing. This page explains what information may be stored and how it is used.

Information Collected

Gachax may store account-related and application-related data such as:

• username or display name
• authentication token
• collection data and card history
• marketplace activity
• profile preferences such as avatar, bio, or banner

How Data Is Used

The information stored by Gachax is used only to provide the core features of the application, including account access, card collection tracking, marketplace features, profile display, and related gameplay companion features.

Cookies and Local Storage

Gachax may use browser local storage or similar technologies to keep users signed in, remember interface settings, and improve the user experience.

Third-Party Services

Gachax may rely on third-party hosting, database, API, and infrastructure providers in order to operate the service.

Card data and artwork references may come from official APIs or other authorized public sources, depending on the supported game.

Data Sharing

Gachax does not sell personal data. Information is only used for the operation of the website and its community features.

Data Removal

If you want your account-related data removed, you may contact the site owner or administrator and request deletion.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated over time as the application evolves.
